Napolitan Breaded Meat

Description

A delicious type of dish. It has become a favourite in Argentinian restaurants, and children love it. To prepare it, you'll need the following:

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of round roast beef.
  • 750g of breadcrumbs.
  • 3 eggs.
  • A bunch of parsley.
  • A clove of garlic.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

    • For the sauce topping

    • 2 tomatoes.
    • A spoonful of olive oil
    • A clove of garlic.
    • Oregan.
    • 3 stems of parsley.
    • Salt and pepper to taste.

    Steps

    Sauce topping

    1. Blend the tomatoes.
    2. Chop the garlic and the parsley.
    3. Simmer the tomate slowly in a pan for 5 minutes.
    4. Add the garlic, the parsley, salt and pepper
    5. Keep simmering for another 5 minutes.

    Beef Preparation

    1. Cut the beef in slices.
    2. Chop a clove of garlic and the parsley.
    3. Scramble the eggs in a bowl and mix them with the parsley, garlic, salt and pepper.
    4. Pour the slices of beef into the scrambled eggs.
    5. Put a lid to the bowl. Let the mix rest for at least 3 hours in the fridge.
    6. Put the breadcrumbs in a platter.
    7. Dip the slices of beef into breadcrumbs.
    8. Deep fry the slices for a minute
    9. Put the slices in an oven platter.
    10. Add the sauce topping and some cheese.
    11. Let them in the oven at 180Â°C until the cheese melts.
    12. Enjoy!
